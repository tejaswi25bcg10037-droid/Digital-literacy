Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

Do’s

1.Think before posting anything online

2.Maintain a professional and respectful tone

3.Protect your personal information using privacy settings

4.Verify information before sharing it

5.Use social media for learning and networking

Don’ts

1.Do not share sensitive personal details publicly

2.Do not post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content

3.Do not believe or spread fake news

4.Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments

5.Do not use weak passwords or ignore account security
